Hi, I'm Anjali.

I'm a product designer based in San Francisco, CA. I'm passionate about creating simple and delightful experiences that empower people to do their best work. Currently, I'm a product design manager at Scribe, where I lead the design team and work closely with PMs and engineers to build a better content creation platform. Previously, I worked at Dropbox, where I designed features for our web and desktop app, and at a small startup called InVision, where I was the first design hire and helped shape the product from the ground up. My background is in visual design, but I've always been interested in the intersection of design and technology. I'm excited to be part of the next wave of innovation and help shape the future of digital products.

Product design is a craft that requires a deep understanding of human behavior, an eye for detail, and a passion for solving complex problems. It's a discipline that combines art and science, intuition and analysis, creativity and logic. I love the challenge of balancing these different elements and creating something that's not just beautiful, but also functional and meaningful. My goal as a designer is to make people's lives easier, more enjoyable, and more productive. I believe that great design has the power to inspire, uplift, and connect us to each other and the world around us. That's why I'm so passionate about what I do, and why I'm always looking for new ways to grow, learn, and push the boundaries of what's possible.
